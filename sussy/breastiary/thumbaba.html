Thumbaba is the severed hadn of the great god of Mesopotatia: Humbaba.
First spotted in the ruins of sumeria, he is filled with evil rage and energy that it uses to power itself despite being severed from a blood supply. It is like a demon on legs.

His biggest rival (asides Gilgamecsh) is Pinkidu, the lone kid named finger of Enkidu. He also has had tough battles with the baby, everyone know s the baby.

He can shoot fingernails from his finger like a gun, use his tongue as a violent whip, and kick you with his fast legs. He is fast, he is strong, he IS coming for you.
